About Me

Hi, I'm Alexander von Bredow. I am 24 years old and originally from Washington D.C. . I obtained my Bachelors of Science in Biology from The university of North Carolina at Wilmington in 2014. After graduating I lived in Seattle for a year working in a trace metals analysis laboratory, before moving to Raleigh at the beginning of 2016.

In my free time I enjoy playing guitar and climbing (both indoor and outdoor). I am currently working on my coding skills and am interested in bioinformatics. I believe that at the crossroads of biology and computer science there is a lot of progress that can be made. Thank you for visiting my page, if you would like to contact me, send me an email.
